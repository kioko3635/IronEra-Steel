About IronEra Steel Ltd

IronEra is a legacy steel manufacturing plant operating in the Kenyan market and part of Ubiquity Group, an industrial platform focused on transforming manufacturing businesses across Africa.

With an established operational footprint and strong market presence, IronEra plays a critical role in supporting Kenya's growing demand for steel across infrastructure, construction, and industrial sectors.

Following its integration into Ubiquity Group, IronEra has undergone a strategic transformation with a strong focus on sustainability, operational stability, and long-term value creation.

At IronEra Steel Ltd, sustainability is embedded in how we manufacture steel. Our operations focus on recycling steel scrap, reducing reliance on primary steel production and supporting a more circular industrial economy.

By producing steel from recycled materials, we contribute to lower carbon emissions, reduced industrial waste, and more efficient use of natural resources.

In addition, IronEra Steel Ltd supports local economic development through:

  • Creating stable industrial employment
  • Supporting local supply chains
  • Strengthening domestic manufacturing capacity
  • Contributing to infrastructure development across Kenya and East Africa

As part of Ubiquity Group's Steel Vertical, IronEra Steel Ltd is committed to building a responsible, efficient, and sustainable steel manufacturing platform that supports long-term industrial growth.
